About BuroTech

Learn about BuroTech and how we streamline administrative processes with technology-driven solutions.

Our Mission

BuroTech was founded to simplify the complex world of administrative processes through technology-driven solutions and expert guidance. We bridge the gap between bureaucratic requirements and practical execution, helping individuals and organizations navigate their administrative needs with confidence and efficiency.

Our Approach

We provide authorized, non-legal support for document preparation, compliance, and workflow management. Our services are designed to help clients navigate complex bureaucratic processes efficiently using structured methodologies and modern technology tools.

What sets us apart is our commitment to accuracy, organization, and leveraging technology to streamline traditionally time-consuming tasks. Whether you're preparing immigration documents, managing business filings, or optimizing administrative workflows, we ensure your documents and processes are properly organized and compliant.

Meet the Founder

Pedro Salvador Guerrero

Founder & Principal Consultant

Pedro Salvador Guerrero brings over seven years of specialized experience in immigration documentation, regulatory compliance, and administrative process management to BuroTech. As a U.S.-Mexican citizen fluent in English and Spanish, Pedro has dedicated his career to helping individuals and organizations navigate complex bureaucratic systems with precision and efficiency.

Currently helping law firms in DC and Virginia with employment-based and humanitarian immigration paralegal matters before USCIS and EOIR, through BuroTech. His expertise includes drafting legal documents, preparing visa petitions, managing client communications, and providing bilingual translation and interpretation services.

Pedro's professional background includes analyzing cases for over 500 visa petitioners from Latinamerica, Europe, and Asia, drafting more than 700 immigration-related documents, and developing optimized workflows using Excel, automation tools, and technology-driven solutions. Previously, he served as a Documentation Officer at the Consular Office of the Mexican Embassy, where he assisted over 1,500 citizens with document processing and regulatory compliance.

Pedro combines deep legal knowledge with practical expertise in document preparation, compliance frameworks, and process optimization. His experience in Mexican government agencies, including the Mexican homolog of the Administration for Children and Families (SNDIF), has equipped him with comprehensive understanding of regulatory analysis and policy implementation.

Pedro founded BuroTech to provide accessible, technology-driven administrative support that simplifies complex processes for individuals and organizations. His commitment to accuracy, organization, and leveraging modern tools ensures clients receive professional guidance throughout their administrative journeys.
